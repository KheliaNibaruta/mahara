Password reminder

If you have forgotten your username or password, the site can send you a message telling you your username. It also provides you with a link where you can reset your password.

Enter your email address that you use for the Pharmacy Council register in the box and click "Send request". You will receive an email automatically. It contains your username and a link to change your password. Follow the link in the email to confirm your password reset request.

Warning: If you receive a password change email that you have not requested, it may be fraudulent. Do not click any links in such emails.
