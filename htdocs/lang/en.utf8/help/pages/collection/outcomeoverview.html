Outcome overview

This page displays all outcomes to be worked on in this portfolio. You can see more details about them and track your progress.

Each outcome can be associated with one or more activity pages that hold evidence, information about the progress through the outcome, and reflections.

How much you can change on this page depends on your role:

  • Group administrators can set up outcomes and make changes to them.
  • Group tutors can perform assessment and add activity pages.
  • Regular group members can add content to activity pages but cannot change content on this 'Outcomes' overview page.
